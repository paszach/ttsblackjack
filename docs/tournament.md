# Tournament Info

- Sessions are held 8:15-9pm after Thursday Rehearsals
- Table spots are first come first serve, so don't miss out on signing up!
- See [Table & Game Rules](rules.md) for all rules + game info
- Everyone starts with 500 Cogs (Legally known as techdollars)
- Players buy in for chips at the start of each session, and cash back out for Cogs at the end
- **Players are in charge of keeping their Cogs and bringing them to table sessions!**
- On the last session of term, everyone's Cogs will be converted into points (1-1)
- Whoever has the most points overall wins the mystery prize (ooooh...)
- Current standings can be seen on the [Leaderboard](https://leaderboardhq.com/66z8uwuv)
